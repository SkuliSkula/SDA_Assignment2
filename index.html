Explain in your own words why you think I want the axes to be the same for both years? (Even though you know how to make axes adapt to the data values.)

Answer: The x- and y-axis do not change when the buttons for different years are clicked, because it really helps to illustrate the difference in change of number of crimes in both categories for each year. If the x- and y-axis would adjust based on the data for each year, the points would not represent the same values in the scatter plot for both years.
